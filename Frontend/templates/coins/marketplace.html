How to start off?

  1.   As beginners, you can top up your wallet, using a test VISA card. You will be using USD as the standard currency.
  2.   To check for prices and trends, you can click on the 6 coins that are available on this page.
  3.   In the individual coins section, you can buy or sell the coins at the price that is being shown on the browser at any point of time.
  4.   To check the real-time balance of your wallet, please click on profile in the navigation bar at the top. It will show the composition of coins and the total value of the wallet at that time.
